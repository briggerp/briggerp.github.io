About Me

Born in the USA and raised between Switzerland and Florida, my journey has been a blend of diverse experiences and cultures. I moved to Switzerland as a baby and relocated to Florida with my family in first grade where I spent five formative years. Moving back to Switzerland in sixth grade, I completed the Matura before taking a gap year to begin my Swiss army service and train at the Ferrero Tennis Academy in Spain. As an optimistic and culturally curious individual, I thrive on exploration. My passions for languages, travel, and sports, especially tennis, fuel my zest for life. This enthusiasm guided me through my studies at ETH Zurich, where I worked on an aerial manipulator project that sparked my interest in robotics. An exchange semester at the National University of Singapore further broadened my horizons. In my free time, I enjoy playing the piano and guitar, engaging in card games with friends and family, and sailing—an activity I am licensed for. Whether on the tennis court or exploring new skills, I approach life with enthusiasm and a positive outlook. Life, to me, is an adventure waiting to unfold.
